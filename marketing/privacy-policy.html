Privacy Policy for Trump

Effective date: April 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Trump (“the App”, “we”, “our”) handles information when you use it on iOS and Android devices.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Trump is designed to minimize data collection. We do not collect, store, or share any of the following:

  • Real names, email addresses, phone numbers, or postal addresses
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, advertising IDs)
  • Precise or approximate location
  • Contacts, photos, calendars, or other personal files
  • Payment or financial information (the App has no in-app purchases)
  • Browsing history or analytics beyond what is described below

2. Information Used Only During Gameplay

When you play multiplayer mode, the App asks you to enter a temporary guest username. This username:

  • Is used only to label your seat at the game table for other players
  • Is held in memory for the duration of the game session
  • Is not stored on any server after the session ends
  • Is not linked to your Apple ID, Google account, or any real identity

Single-player mode does not require a username or any input beyond gameplay.

3. Network Communication (Multiplayer Only)

Multiplayer connects your device to a dedicated game server we operate. During a session the server temporarily handles:

  • Your chosen guest username
  • Your IP address (as required by any internet connection)
  • In-game actions you send (card plays, trump suit selection, chat, etc.)
  • The 6-character room code you create or join

This information is kept in server memory only for the life of the room and is discarded when the room closes. We do not maintain long-term logs that identify individual players.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. It uses only standard iOS and Android platform APIs and Apple’s App Store / Google Play distribution systems.

6. Data Security

Multiplayer traffic between your device and our server uses standard network protocols. While no internet transmission is 100% secure, we use reasonable measures to protect in-transit gameplay data.

7. Your Rights

Because the App does not store personal data, there is typically nothing to access, export, or delete. If you have questions or concerns about your privacy while using Trump, contact us at the email below and we will respond as quickly as possible.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
