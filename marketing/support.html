How to Play

Trump is a 4-player trick-taking card game. You and your partner (sitting across from you) play against two opponents. Each round, one player picks the trump suit and leads the first trick. First team to win 7 tricks (books) wins the round.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a multiplayer game with friends?

From the main menu, choose Multiplayer, enter a guest username, then tap Create Room. Share the 6-character room code with your friends so they can join from their own devices. The host can start the game with 2, 3, or 4 real players — empty seats are filled with AI.

Why does an AI take over my seat sometimes?

In multiplayer, each player has 60 seconds to play a card. If the turn timer expires or you disconnect, the AI plays for you so the game keeps moving. You regain control on your next turn as soon as you tap any card.

Can I rejoin a game after I disconnect?

Yes. As long as the room is still active, enter the same room code to rejoin. The AI will hand control back to you on your next turn.

Does the game work between iOS and Android?

Yes. Trump supports full crossplay between iOS and Android devices in the same room.

Why can’t I play a specific card?

Cards that can’t legally be played this turn are dimmed. You must follow the suit that was led if you have a card of that suit. If you don’t, you may play any card, including trump.

Are there in-app purchases or ads?

No. Trump is completely free with no ads, no in-app purchases, and no subscription.
